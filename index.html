About

Mouse Rat! Is a rock band based out of Pawnee Indiana. Formed by guitarist and singer Andy Dwyer, Mouse Rat is comprised of some of the best musical talent the area has to offer and strives to bring passion and excellence to every show and recording.

Mouse Rat

Mouse Rat is the most popular rock band in Indiana. They have headlined several iconic events such as The Harvest Festival and The Unity Concert. Although Mouse Rat! No longer makes music as a band several of their members have gone on to do other amazing things. Andy, became the popular childrens star Johnny Karate! He even served as security on several political campaigns.

Buy Our CD's Check out Johnny Karate!

The Harvest Festival is an annual event held by the Parks and Recreation department. The returning featured guest this year is everyones favorite. Lil' Sebastian

The Unity Concert commemorates a historical moment in Pawnee history that celebrates the merger between the former rival sister ciies Pawnee and Eagleton Indiana.

Before Mouse Rat! We used to go by several other names. ScareCrow Boat, Just The Tip, Rat Mouse, Tackle Shaft, Handrail Suicide and countless others.
